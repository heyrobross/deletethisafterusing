Key Takeaways
What to Remember
01
VCs hunt outliers, not good companies. One 100× return makes the fund.
02
Momentum beats current numbers. 25% MoM at $200K > 5% MoM at $500K.
03
Discovery is where VCs compete. 6 months early = 6× valuation difference.
04
For AI tools, the moat question is existential: "Why won't OpenAI build this?"
05
Leading indicators give you the edge. GitHub velocity predicts revenue 6 months early.
